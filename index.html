Sacred Birman

Appearance at a glance:

Semi-long, silky coat in a cream “colour-point” pattern with dark mask, ears, tail, and legs; bright sapphire-blue eyes; pure-white “gloves” on all four paws

Typical temperament:

Calm, gentle, affectionate but not overly vocal

Origin story:

Legend traces them to Burmese temples; the modern breed was established in France in the 1920s
